About me

Hello! My name is Priyankan Kirupaharan and I am a 2nd-year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Rhode Island. Currently, I am pursuing my academic journey with a keen focus on Virtual Reality, A11y and HCI4D. I have the privilege of being guided by Prof. Krishna Venkatasubramanian and also been lucky to collaborate with Dr. Tina-Marie Ranalli .

I did my undergraduate at University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka with major and honors in Computer Science and minors in Mathematics and Physics. I have industrial experience as software developer for 4 years developing enterprise application in Spring boot.
