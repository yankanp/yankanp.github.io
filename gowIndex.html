Dr. Gowthami Mahendran

Innovator in Molecular Biology, RNA Therapeutics, and Precision Medicine

Dr. Gowthami Mahendran is an emerging thought leader in molecular biology, with research spanning RNA therapeutics, mitochondrial biochemistry, and precision medicine. Her work holds significant promise for advancing targeted treatments for genetic disorders and improving disease outcomes in areas such as cardiac and neurodevelopmental diseases. Funders are invited to explore and support Dr. Mahendran's groundbreaking research, which has the potential to transform healthcare and inspire next-generation therapies.

Research Areas

  • RNA-Based Therapeutics: Developing RNA regulation strategies for therapeutic applications in cancer and genetic disorders.
  • Precision Medicine: Multi-omics integration to tailor treatment plans for conditions like Miller-Dieker Syndrome and other rare genetic diseases.
  • Gene Editing and CRISPR: Leveraging CRISPR technology for targeted gene modifications with potential clinical applications.
  • Cardiac and Mitochondrial Genetics: Investigating mitochondrial function in cardiac morphogenesis and disease.

Impactful Projects

  • RNA Regulation in Bacterial Systems: Studying RNA regulation to understand and control bacterial gene expression, contributing to new antibacterial strategies.
  • MALAT1 Therapeutic Modulation: Exploring Locked Nucleic Acid (LNA) oligonucleotides to reduce MALAT1 levels, a potential pathway for cancer therapies.
  • Multi-Omics Analysis in Miller-Dieker Syndrome: Uncovering genetic and protein-level changes to improve diagnosis and therapy for rare genetic disorders.
